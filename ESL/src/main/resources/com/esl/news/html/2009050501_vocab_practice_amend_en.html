Adjust difficulties of Phonetic Practice / Vocabulary Practice

According to opinions from our members, the difficulties of phonetic practice are too high for children. Therefore, we have adjusted the difficulty for all level phonetic practice.

In addition, Phonetic Practice is renamed to Vocab Practice to express this function are not only useful to children learning phonetic but also for learner who want to acquire new English wording. The Vocab Practice is helpful to children who know phonetic or not. Later, a new function, 'One day, one word', to helping children remember vocabulary will implemented very soon.
