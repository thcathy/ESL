You got a new practice to-do: "Phonics Practice"

Fun Fun Spell give you another English practice "Phonics Practice" for those who would like to learn the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA). Same as the previous practice type, the wording are separated into different grades. Moreover, you can choose level of practice before starting. No matter you have knowledge on IPA or not, there should be a level suitable.

The lowest level, "Rookie", would suggest the phonics of the question only. Therefore, what you need to do is re-ordering those symbols. That is most suitable for user who have not learn IPA before.

For those who have learn IPA, you can try the "Full" level. All phonics symbol will present for selection. And you score in full level is presented in ranking table. So, try you best to answer correctly.
