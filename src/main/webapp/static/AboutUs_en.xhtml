Fun Fun Spell is a website provided free English practice launched on January 2009.
It is produced by two volunteers, a English teacher father and a Software Engineer son in Hong Kong.
As we are working as a community project, with limited time resources, Fun Fun Spell are keep on improving and developing in discontinuously.

And your support is the major motivation for our development!

Up to now, we already provided:

  1. Vocabulary practice - Learning new words or improving listening
  2. Phonics practice - Practicing International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)
  3. Self dictation - Pronouncing any word you input for dictation yourself
