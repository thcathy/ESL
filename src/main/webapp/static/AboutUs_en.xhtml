Fun Fun Spell provides free English practice. It launched on January 2009.
It is produced by two volunteers in Hong Kong, an father who teach English and a son who is a Software Engineer.
As we are working as a community project, with limited time resources, we will try out best to improve and develop Fun Fun Spell.

And your support is the major motivation for our development!

Up to now, we are providing:

  1. Vocabulary practice - Learning new words or improving listening
  2. Phonics practice - Practicing International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)
  3. Self dictation - Pronouncing any word you input for dictation yourself
